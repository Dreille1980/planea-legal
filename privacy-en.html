Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 16th, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Planea. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

2. Data Collection

Planea collects the following types of data:

  • Profile information (name, dietary preferences)
  • App usage data
  • Family member information
  • Meal and recipe preferences

3. Data Usage

We use your data to:

  • Generate personalized meal plans
  • Improve user experience
  • Provide customer support
  • Manage your subscription

4. Storage and Security

Your data is stored locally on your device and securely synchronized with our servers. We use industry-standard encryption protocols to protect your information.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties, except:

  • With your explicit consent
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • With our service providers (hosting, payment processing)

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Modify or delete your data
  • Withdraw your consent
  • Export your data

7. Cookies and Similar Technologies

The app uses local storage technologies to enhance your user experience.

8. Policy Changes

We may update this privacy policy. Significant changes will be communicated to you.
